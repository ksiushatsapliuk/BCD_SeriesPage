Season 1

In the first season, the characters are divided into three timelines. Here are the main characters and their surroundings:

1963: The Stanton Family

  • Beth Ann Stanton is an ideal housewife whose life revolves around her husband.
  • Rob Stanton is her husband, a successful specialist who treats his wife more like a servant and has affairs on the side.
  • April Warner is a young waitress and Rob's lover, with whom Beth Ann forms a secret friendship.
  • Sheila Mosconi is a sharp-tongued neighbor and Beth Ann's only true friend.

1984: The Grove family

  • Simona Grove is a rich, glamorous socialite, for whom image is above all else.
  • Carl Grove is Simona's third husband, who hid his homosexuality for years.
  • Tommy Hart is the 18-year-old son of Simona's best friend, who has been in love with her since childhood.
  • Naomi Hart is Tommy's mother and Simone's friend, who begins to suspect something is wrong.

2019: The Harding-Cohen family

  • Taylor Harding is a successful lawyer and feminist who practices open marriage.
  • Eli Cohen is Taylor's husband, a screenwriter who is in a creative crisis.
  • Jade / Irene is Taylor's lover, who moves into their house and soon charms Eli.

Season 2

In the second season (1949), the characters are connected in one common web of intrigues and murders:

The Fillcote family

  • Alma Fillcot is the main character. A modest housewife and gardening lover. The desire for social recognition turns her from an innocent woman into a cold-blooded criminal.
  • Bertrand Fillcot is Alma's husband, a good-natured veterinarian. He keeps a dark secret: he is a "merciful" serial killer who "helps" the hopelessly ill to get out of life.
  • Dee Fillcot is the daughter of Alma and Bertrand. The only truly honest and moral character of the season.

Secular society and its environment

  • Rita Castillo is the president of the garden club and Alma's main antagonist. A cruel and glamorous woman who is waiting for the death of her rich husband.
  • Vern Loomis is a private detective and war veteran whom Rita hires to follow Scooter. Later becomes an ally of Dy.
  • Scooter Stripes is a handsome but short-sighted novice actor. He is Rita's lover (for the sake of money) and at the same time secretly meets Dy.
  • Carlo Castillo is Rita's elderly and paralyzed husband.
  • Katherine Castillo is Rita's stepdaughter. He hates his stepmother and comes to leave her inheritance and lead her to clean water.

Secondary, but important heroes

  • Grace and Joan are arrogant members of the garden club, whose approval is so important to Alma.
